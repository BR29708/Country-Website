Mount Rushmore

Mount Rushmore is a monument to some of the greatest presidents of the past. The figures of George Washington, Thomas Jefferson, Theodore Rosevelt, and Abraham Lincon are carved into a stone cliffside in Keystone, South Dakota. Each face is about 60 feet tall, and their noses are no longer than 20 feet. Mount Rushmore brings visitors to remember the great heritage of the United States.
