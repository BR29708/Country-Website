The Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty is a birthday gift from France to the USA. Given on July 4th, 1884, the statue of liberty stands at a whopping 305 feet tall! The statue of liberty is made of copper, so originally, it wasn't green. However, over time, the statue rusted causing the green coating we all see today. As new American citizens sail over the atlantic and enter America for the first time, the Statue of Liberty stands as a symbol for freedom, and a beginning to a new life.
